My name is Sally Soleimani, and I’m a multidisciplinary designer with industrial design background based in Toronto, Canada. I started as an industrial designer and graduated in 2013 from OCAD University. I’m fascinated by designing new products and creating unique concepts. So far, I worked on cooperation projects with companies such as Interior manufacturing Group (IMG), Array marketing and Central Group as well as some personal projects on the side. For the past few years, I developed diverse skills such as UX design and visual design and graduated from Brainstation in 2019. I have taken Product Management courses to become more familiar with different tools and techniques needed to successfully coordinate all aspects of product development.

If you want to chat, work together, or just want to be nice and leave some feedback, drop a line. I enjoy notifications!
