Ace Bakery - 3 Way shoppable

December 2021 - The Central Group

I created a dynamic 3-way shoppable unit for Ace Bakery, blending metal and wire shelves, a double-sided header and a mobile design (18” x 18” x 60”). My role was to present multiple concepts to the client, refine the chosen one, and finally translate it into precise engineering drawings. The result is an innovative, visually appealing and functional display solution that enhances the overall retail experience for Ace Bakery and its customers.

Check out the case study

Software: Solidworks, Illustrator

Timeline: Entire project 8 months, Just the fixture 6 weeks

My Role: Design, Present, Engineer, Render, Final Product Quality Control

GLab Touch and Learn

September 2023 - The Central Group

The GLab Touch and Learn project aimed to create a unique display unit for peg board walls on Walmart Shelves, showcasing the latest GLab razors for customer interaction. I designed with focus on both strength and safety, the challenge was to prevent customer interference while ensuring secure shipping. It was critical to be mindful of cost. The unit combined durable metal for structural strength and styrene for graphics, providing a visually engaging and secure solution. The innovative signed allowed customers to touch and experience the newest razors, which featured a swirling capability, enhancing the overall retail engagement.

Software: Solidworks

Timeline: 6 months

My Role: Design, Engineer, Render
